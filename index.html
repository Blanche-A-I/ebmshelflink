How it works

Four steps to your ShelfLink

Send us your products or specials. We do the rest — you get a branded, shareable link your customers can browse and act on.

1

Send us your products or specials

Share your product list, prices, and specials via WhatsApp or email — however works for you.

2

We build your branded ShelfLink page

We create your mobile-friendly, branded product page — ready for your review before it goes live.

3

Share your link with customers

Once approved, share it via WhatsApp, Facebook, QR code, or any channel your customers use.

4

They browse and enquire or order on WhatsApp

Customers tap to enquire or order directly through WhatsApp — no app, no login, no friction.
